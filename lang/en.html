About Kobo Button

Kobo Button is unofficial and fan made website to play audio clip of Kobo from various sources. The types of audio available are words said by Kobo, sounds such as laughing and sneezing, and songs (if enabled via the settings above).

Thanks to the contributors involved in the development and maintenance of this website and` special thanks to Ryuna for the Kobo art.
